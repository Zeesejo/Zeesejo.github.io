WELCOME TO EDMUND FLYING CLUB


Edmund Flying School has been training pilots since 1952 and is recognised internationally as a quality facility, providing consistently high standards in all aspects of flight training.

Edmund Flying School provides international and domestic students with a solid foundation in all the critical areas of aviation theory and pilot training skills, and also teaches you how to work effectively in the constantly changing environment of the skies. Our fully integrated pilot training programs transform students into strong, decisive, skilled pilots capable of operating in the commercial and corporate sector.
